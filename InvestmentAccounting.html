🇨🇦 Investment Accounting Services in Canada

Whether you're managing personal wealth, overseeing a corporate portfolio, or handling investment funds on behalf of clients, precise accounting is key to making confident, data-driven decisions. At nextledgers, we offer specialized investment accounting services tailored to the needs of Canadian investors and firms — helping you optimize returns, minimize risks, and stay fully compliant with national standards.

📊 What Is Investment Accounting?

  • Tracking, valuation, and reporting of investments (stocks, bonds, mutual funds, REITs, private placements, alt assets)
  • Monitors portfolio performance, real-time gains/losses, CRA compliance
  • Supports buy-sell-hold decisions with clarity

🧠 Who Needs Investment Accounting?

  • High-net-worth individuals and family offices
  • Wealth managers and portfolio managers
  • Corporations with reserve capital or benefit plans
  • Real estate investment entities
  • CPA firms outsourcing investment accounting

📦 Our Core Investment Accounting Services

  • 1. Portfolio Valuation & Performance Reporting: Book vs market value, gains/losses, dividends, distributions, capital events, and benchmarking. Works with Questrade, RBC, TD, etc.
  • 2. Asset Allocation & Rebalancing: Equity/fixed income/alt allocation, domestic vs international, sectoral exposure, rebalancing insights.
  • 3. Risk & Compliance Monitoring: Volatility, drawdown, liquidity, duration, CRA passive income rules, overconcentration alerts.
  • 4. Investment Income & Capital Gains Reconciliation: T3, T5, FX tracking, ACB calc, reinvested divs, return of capital, private equity and real estate logs.
  • 5. CRA and Audit-Ready Investment Reporting: Schedule 7/125/50, holding companies, trust T3s, T1135s, partnership interest logs, foreign disclosures.
